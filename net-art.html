the web "test"

date: 10/28/2019

this website was created to demonstrate how the internet is a source of "surveillance" as it tracks what you do based on the input information you give it. this website also embodies a few internet "trends" such as memes and asmr. in its own way, it is a form of "net art". as it allows its audience to actively interact with it. it was sort of difficult trying to figure out what to put on each page, but as I kept working with it, it turned into something very "playful" and "fun." there are a total of 4 outcomes when taking the "test."

visit the actual page @ " https://acmercado.github.io/critical-interactivity/index.html. "
