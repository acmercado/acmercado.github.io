Net Art

"The Closet"

"The Closet" tells a story about a girl and her past with relationships which gets her thinking, "What should I do?"

I wanted to make sure that my audience understand that it's not always easy to "come out of the closet" and explain it through someone's personal experience.

At the ending of the "game," I included websites that will help inform the "player" of subjects used to create the overall story of the main character

I used JPGs and MP4s as visuals. I also embedded music in the background for some choices.

To create the visuals, I used Adobe Illustrator to create the backgrounds, and Adobe Photoshop to add the JPGs of the silhouttes onto it.

I also used Adobe Photoshop to create the MP4s.
