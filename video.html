Video + Sound

"Remembering You"

"Tell a story, without telling a story."

My video was originally supposed to be someone who goes into depression and eventually drives himself to commit suicide,

but as I was editing my project even more, I decided to change my theme to someone who lost their loved one due to a car accident.

The video itself would seem like it is about a boy who is remembering the good times he had with his girlfriend, and has now broken up with her.

With the sound, however, it adds a more emotional aspect and changes the story to the boy losing their loved one due to a car accident.
