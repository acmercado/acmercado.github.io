Remix Photo

"Ignorance"

I wanted to make a Remix that showed one of the negative things about our society, thus creating "Ignorance."

My Remix art is based on the fact that we, as humans, have been destroying the natural life that once used to surround us

and act 'ignorant' to this for the living styles that we created for ourselves.

To create this artwork, I used different pictures and tried to blend them together using PhotoShop in order to make it look like one picture.

This was my first time using PhotoShop, so I hope to improve my skills in the near future.
